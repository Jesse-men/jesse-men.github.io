How it works:

  • The list above comes from _data/reading_papers.yml; edit that file in the repo to add or remove papers.
  • Use "Add paper" to save papers in your browser (localStorage); they persist after refresh.
  • Use "Export YAML" to copy locally saved papers as YAML, then paste under papers: in _data/reading_papers.yml to sync to the site.
  • Bookmark this page and paste the paper URL into the add form for quick recording.
